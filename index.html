Quick Tips

Content Tips You Can Use Today

Short, practical advice from the Framework Media team. No fluff, no theory — just actionable steps you can apply immediately.

01

Your hook matters more than your content

If the first 3 seconds don't grab attention, the rest doesn't matter. Start with a bold statement, a question, or a counterintuitive idea.

02

Retention beats reach

Getting 100K views with 10% retention isn't as valuable as 20K views with 60% retention. Focus on keeping people watching.

03

System beats strategy

Having a plan is good — having a repeatable system is better. Build a workflow, test it, measure it, and repeat what works.
